Okavango Delta, Botswana

This picture was taken in the Okavango Delta of Botswana. The delta is home to more than "200,000 large mammals" (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Okavango_Delta)

My favorite part about Botswana is the country's nature conservation laws. Hunting is illegal in the entire country and a lot of the land is nationally protected.
